## STAR story

**Situation:** Multi-agent workflows can look impressive while hiding unbounded
execution and unvalidated handoffs.

**Task:** Build a repository-analysis workflow that is explainable, testable and
safe to inspect as a portfolio project.

**Action:** Kept execution authority in deterministic code, validated sender and
receiver payloads, bounded calls/tokens/revisions/artifact size with RuntimeGuard,
and preserved a legacy pipeline as an A/B baseline. Added a committed mock
benchmark and a credential-free artifact demo.

**Result:** The portfolio candidate has 12/12 passing mock contract cases, a
reviewed Draft PR, and 660 passing automated tests. The benchmark is explicitly
not presented as live model-quality or cost evidence.
